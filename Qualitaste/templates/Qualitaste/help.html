Help & FAQs

How to Order

You can place an order by visiting the Order page, selecting your cake preferences, and filling out the form. A confirmation will be sent to your email.

Transport Charges

Transport charges vary based on your delivery location. Orders within Kampala are charged at a flat rate, while outside deliveries may incur additional costs.

Packaging

All our cakes are securely packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Custom packaging is available upon request.

Our Pricing

Prices vary depending on the cake type, size, and customization. Please refer to our Gallery page for typical pricing or contact us for custom quotes.

Order Cancellation & Refunds

Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of placement. Refunds are processed based on our policy outlined in the Terms and Conditions.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out via email, phone, or WhatsApp. Our team is happy to help!
